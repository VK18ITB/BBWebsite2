Fee Structure

Grade Amount

Class Schedule

- Classes are conducted from 4:30 pm to 5:30 pm and 5:30 pm to 6:30 pm (two batches a day), Monday to Thursday.
- There are 4 to 5 students in one batch.
- Classes are conducted twice a week for each student.
- All batches are for 1 hour.
- Students can pick their slot and confirm the availability by contacting me.
- During exams, extra classes can be conducted without additional charges.
- Classes can be conducted both offline and online depending on your requirements.
